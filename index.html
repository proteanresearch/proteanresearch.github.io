Protean Research Inc.

Advancing Oncology Diagnostics

Protean Research Inc. is dedicated to developing innovative diagnostics that inform the selection of combination therapies for cancer treatment. Our mission is to address the challenge of drug escape in oncology, where cancer cells adapt and become resistant to single-agent therapies.

With a large number of new oncology therapies expected to be approved in the coming years, selecting the most effective treatment combinations is crucial for improving patient outcomes. However, current diagnostic approaches have not kept pace with the rapid advancements in analytical techniques and the emergence of new technologies over the past decade.

At Protean Research, we are developing new assays and workflows using existing analytical tools to provide oncologists with the information they need to make informed decisions about combination therapies. Our goal is to enhance the precision of cancer treatment and improve patient care.

Based in Los Angeles, Protean Research Inc. is positioned at the forefront of oncology diagnostics innovation.
