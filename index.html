About Us

Protean Research Inc. is dedicated to developing novel diagnostics to inform the selection of combination therapies for cancer patients. Our mission is to enable clinicians to optimize treatment strategies by matching each patient to the most effective blend of therapies, accelerating progress toward better outcomes for all.

The Challenge

Drug escape in oncology is a major hurdle. Cancer cells evolve quickly and can become resistant to single-agent therapies, rendering once-effective treatments less successful over time. The rapid development and approval of new oncology drugs – with many more in the pipeline – make it increasingly difficult to determine which therapies or combinations will deliver the best results.

Diagnostic approaches have not kept pace with the explosion of new treatments and sophisticated analytical technologies that have emerged in the last two decades. While large resources are devoted to drug discovery and drug development, comparatively fewer efforts focus on guiding clinicians and patients toward the right combination regimens. This gap in innovative diagnostic solutions perpetuates suboptimal patient outcomes and limits the potential of cutting-edge therapies.

Our Approach

At Protean Research, we believe that the most impactful way to improve patient outcomes is by pairing individuals with the best combination of therapies. Unlike many manufacturers who focus on companion diagnostics for their specific drug candidates, our cross-cancer, universal approach aims to guide therapy selection at a broader level, ensuring that every patient receives a rational, data-driven combination of treatments.

Our platform integrates cutting-edge molecular diagnostics, advanced analytics, and a systematic evaluation of existing and emerging therapeutics. By partnering with leading academic institutions, clinical centers, and pharmaceutical innovators, we are creating a powerful diagnostic ecosystem that can keep pace with the rapidly evolving oncology landscape.
